Salvijus Misevičius
b. 1999
Lives and works in Vilnius, Lithuania
2018-23 Vilnius Academy of Arts, B.A. in Photography and Media Art
S.M. is familiar with moving image and sound techniques and expresses ideas primarily in those two domains. He works with blocks of movement/duration and bricks of frequency/loudness. Employing associative strategies and structural experiments he explores liminal spaces between real and fictional aspects of history.
